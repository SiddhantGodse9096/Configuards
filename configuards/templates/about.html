About Our Services

🔒 AWS Security Configuration Monitoring

Configuards provides comprehensive security monitoring for your AWS infrastructure. We continuously scan and analyze your cloud resources to identify misconfigurations and security vulnerabilities.

📊 Real-time Security Findings

Get instant alerts on critical security issues across your AWS services including EC2, S3, RDS, Lambda, VPC, CloudFront, IAM, and DynamoDB. Our platform prioritizes findings by severity to help you focus on what matters most.

📈 Compliance Reporting

Generate detailed compliance reports to meet industry standards and regulatory requirements. Track your security posture over time with comprehensive analytics and insights.

⚡ Automated Security Scans

Schedule automated security scans to continuously monitor your AWS environment. Receive notifications when new vulnerabilities are detected and track remediation progress.

🎯 Best Practices Recommendations

Get actionable recommendations based on AWS security best practices. Our platform helps you implement security controls and maintain a strong security posture.

📋 Resource Inventory Management

Maintain a complete inventory of your AWS resources with detailed security assessments. Track changes and monitor resource configurations across your entire infrastructure.
